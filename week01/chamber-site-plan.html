Site Name

Queen Creek Chamber of Commerce. This name represenets a town in which the chamber operates.

Site Purpose

The main purpose of this site is to provide information about Queen Creek’s Chamber of Commerce. The Chamber seeks to build a strong community in the town of Queen Creek by supporting and strengthening its businesses. The site will serve to connect local business-owners with each other, as well as with the town’s council, residents, and visitors.

Another purpose of this site is to enable users to join the chamber, keep up with events, read about the town's history, and explore what Queen Creek has to offer.

  • Is there a local bike shop?

  • What is the history of Queen Creek?
